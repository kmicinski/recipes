## Instructions

1. Slice flank steak against the grain into bite-size strips or cubes. Pat dry and season with salt and black pepper.
2. Set Instant Pot to Saute (High). Add olive oil and sear steak in 2-3 batches until browned on the edges; transfer to a plate.
3. Add onion, carrots, celery, and mushrooms to the pot. Cook 5-6 minutes until softened.
4. Stir in garlic, tomato paste, chipotle peppers in adobo, cocoa powder, smoked paprika, cumin, and thyme. Cook 45 seconds until fragrant.
5. Add a splash of the broth and scrape up all browned bits from the bottom. Add remaining broth, crushed tomatoes, coffee, soy sauce, and worcestershire sauce. Return steak and juices.
6. Lock lid, set valve to Sealing, and pressure cook on High for 30 minutes. Let pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick release remaining pressure.
7. Open lid, stir in cannellini beans, then set to Saute (Low) for 5-8 minutes to thicken slightly.
8. Turn off heat and stir in apple cider vinegar. Taste and adjust salt/pepper. Finish with parsley.
9. Meal-prep/freezer method: cool completely, portion into airtight containers, and freeze up to 3 months. Reheat from thawed on stovetop or in microwave until piping hot.
