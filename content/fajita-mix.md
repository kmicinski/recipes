  - name: poblano peppers

  - name: red onion
    qty: 1
    unit: large
  - name: corn
    qty: 2
    unit: ears
---

## Instructions

1. Oil and season all vegetables.
2. Grill at 450F until charred on all sides.
3. Let cool enough to handle.
4. Slice peppers and onion. Cut corn off the cob.
5. Mix everything together.
6. Freeze in molds.
