## Instructions

1. Cook ground turkey in a large pot until browned. Remove and set aside.
2. Toast dried chillis in the same pot until fragrant. Remove and set aside.
3. Cook onion, green pepper, and garlic until softened.
4. Deglaze with tomato sauce and chicken bone broth.
5. Add torn corn tortillas to the liquid and blend until smooth.
6. Add oregano and return turkey to the pot. Simmer to combine.
7. Freeze in molds overnight.
