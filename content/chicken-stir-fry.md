## Instructions

1. Cook rice according to package directions.
2. Cut chicken into thin strips, season with half the soy sauce.
3. Heat sesame oil in a wok over high heat.
4. Cook chicken until golden, about 5 minutes. Remove and set aside.
5. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds.
6. Add bell pepper and broccoli, stir-fry 3-4 minutes.
7. Return chicken, add remaining soy sauce, toss to combine.
8. Serve over rice.
