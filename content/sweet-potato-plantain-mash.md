  - name: salt
    qty: 2

## Instructions

1. Chop sweet potatoes and plantains into even pieces.
2. Season with grapeseed oil and salt.
3. Air fry at 450F for 20 minutes, toss, then continue for another 20 minutes.
4. Mash to your preferred texture.
5. Freeze in molds.
