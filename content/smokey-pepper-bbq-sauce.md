## Instructions

1. Cook onion and garlic until softened.
2. Toast dried chillis (ancho, cascabel, arbol) until fragrant.
3. Add ketchup, molasses, and vinegar. Stir to combine.
4. Reduce for 5-10 minutes.
5. Blend until completely smooth.
6. Thin with chicken broth to desired consistency.
7. Season with salt to taste.
8. Bottle and store.
