## Instructions

1. Rinse brown rice until water runs mostly clear.
2. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium. Add diced onion and cook 4-5 minutes until softened.
3. Add garlic, cumin, and green chiles; cook 30 seconds.
4. Stir in rice, broth, and salt. Bring to a boil, then cover and cook on low for 40-45 minutes until tender.
5. Rest covered 10 minutes, then fluff. Fold in lime zest, lime juice, and chopped cilantro.
6. Taste and adjust salt.
7. Portion into 14 side servings. Cool fully before sealing containers.
8. Reheat with a splash of water or broth. Freeze up to 3 months for meal prep.
