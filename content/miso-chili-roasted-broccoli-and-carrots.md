## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425 F. Line 2 sheet pans with parchment.
2. Whisk olive oil, white miso, soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, chili crisp, garlic powder, ground ginger, and black pepper into a smooth dressing.
3. Toss broccoli, sliced carrots, and sliced red onion with the dressing until evenly coated.
4. Spread vegetables across both pans in a single layer. Roast 18-24 minutes, rotating pans halfway, until edges are charred and vegetables are tender-crisp.
5. Transfer to a large bowl and toss with lime juice.
6. Finish with toasted sesame seeds and sliced scallions.
7. Meal-prep tip: portion into 14 side servings and refrigerate up to 5 days. For longer storage, freeze up to 2 months and reheat in skillet or microwave until hot.
