## Instructions

1. Cook spaghetti in well-salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
2. While pasta cooks, thinly slice garlic.
3. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-low heat.
4. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, cook until garlic is golden (not brown), about 2 minutes.
5. Add drained pasta to the pan with a splash of pasta water.
6. Toss vigorously, adding more pasta water as needed to create a light sauce.
7. Remove from heat, add parsley and parmesan. Toss and serve immediately.
