## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425 F. Peel and cube sweet potatoes. Toss with olive oil, half the salt, and black pepper.
2. Roast on a sheet pan 30-35 minutes, turning once, until very tender and lightly charred at edges.
3. Transfer to a large bowl and mash to desired texture.
4. Stir in drained black beans, garlic powder, smoked paprika, cumin, remaining salt, and lime juice.
5. Warm briefly on low heat (stovetop or microwave) to meld flavors, then fold in chopped cilantro.
6. Taste and adjust salt/lime.
7. Portion into 14 side servings and cool completely before sealing.
8. Reheat gently and freeze up to 3 months; this mash reheats well with a splash of water if needed.
