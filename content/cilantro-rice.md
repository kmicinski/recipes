    qty: 2
    unit: cups

## Instructions

1. Rinse rice thoroughly until water runs clear.
2. Cook rice in a rice cooker with chicken broth and water.
3. Let rice cool slightly.
4. Chop cilantro and fold into rice along with lime juice.
5. Freeze in molds.
