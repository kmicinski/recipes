## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 400 F. Line a 9x13-inch pan with parchment and lightly grease.
2. Whisk cornmeal, flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and sugar in a large bowl.
3. In another bowl, whisk eggs, buttermilk, and melted butter.
4. Fold wet mixture into dry until just combined, then fold in cheddar, diced jalapeno, and sliced scallions.
5. Spread batter in pan and bake 22-26 minutes until golden and a tester comes out clean.
6. Cool fully, cut into 14 squares, and portion for meal prep.
7. Reheat from refrigerated: microwave 30-45 seconds. Reheat from frozen: microwave 60-90 seconds or warm in 350 F oven for 10 minutes.
8. Freezer tip: wrap squares individually or separate layers with parchment and freeze up to 3 months.
