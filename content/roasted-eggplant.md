## Instructions

1. Roast whole eggplants until completely soft and collapsed.
2. Place in a covered bowl to steam for 10 minutes, then peel.
3. Process eggplant flesh with chickpeas, cumin, smoked paprika, tahini, and salt.
4. Finish with a drizzle of olive oil.
5. Freeze in molds.
