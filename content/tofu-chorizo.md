## Instructions

1. Saut onion and garlic in coconut oil until softened.
2. Add crumbled tofu and cook until liquid evaporates.
3. Add chipotle pepper, adobo sauce, dried chili seasoning, soy sauce, and maple syrup.
4. Cook until tofu is crispy, stirring occasionally.
5. Season with salt to taste.
6. Freeze in molds.
