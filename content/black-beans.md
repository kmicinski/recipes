## Instructions

1. Add dried black beans, onion, garlic, ginger, and deseeded jalapeno to a pressure cooker.
2. Cover with water by 2 inches.
3. Pressure cook for 18 minutes.
4. Season to taste with salt.
5. Optionally blend a portion for a creamier texture.
6. If too thin, reduce on the stove until desired consistency.
7. Freeze in molds.
