## Instructions

1. Sear chicken thighs skin-side down in a hot pan until golden and crispy.
2. Blend tomatillos, onion, garlic, deseeded jalapeno, mint, cilantro, lime juice, and salt until smooth.
3. Flip chicken thighs.
4. Pour salsa verde over the chicken.
5. Simmer 20-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through and tender.
6. Freeze in molds.
