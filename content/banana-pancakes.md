## Instructions

1. Mash bananas in a bowl.
2. Beat in eggs, then add milk.
3. Mix flour and baking powder, fold into wet ingredients until just combined.
4. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
5. Pour small rounds of batter, cook until bubbles form on surface, then flip.
6. Cook another 1-2 minutes until golden.
7. Serve with maple syrup.
