## Instructions

1. Toast farro in olive oil until fragrant.
2. Blend tomatoes, onion, and garlic until smooth.
3. Add tomato blend and chicken broth to the farro.
4. Cook 15-20 minutes until farro is tender and liquid is absorbed.
5. Strain any excess liquid if needed.
6. Freeze in molds.
