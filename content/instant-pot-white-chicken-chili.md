## Instructions

1. Set the Instant Pot to Saute (High). Add olive oil, then onion and jalapeno with a pinch of the salt. Cook 4-5 minutes until softened.
2. Add garlic, cumin, oregano, coriander, chili powder, and smoked paprika. Stir 30 seconds until fragrant.
3. Pour in a splash of broth and scrape up any browned bits. Add remaining broth, chicken, diced green chiles, beans, corn, remaining salt, and white pepper.
4. Lock the lid and pressure cook on High for 12 minutes. Let pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick release the rest.
5. Remove chicken, shred, and return it to the pot.
6. With the pot on Keep Warm, stir in cream cheese until fully melted and silky. Stir in sour cream and lime juice.
7. Taste and adjust seasoning. For a thicker chili, mash some beans against the side of the pot and stir.
8. Serve hot topped with cilantro and monterey jack cheese.
