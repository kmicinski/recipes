## Instructions

1. Blend egg yolk, egg, vinegar, mustard, garlic, and salt until combined.
2. With the blender running, slowly drizzle in avocado oil until fully emulsified.
3. Remove half of the mixture and set aside.
4. Add cilantro leaves to the remaining mixture in the blender and blend until smooth and green.
5. Recombine both halves and mix well.
6. Bottle and store.
