## Instructions

1. Quarter the napa cabbages lengthwise.
2. Sear cabbage quarters on both cut sides until charred.
3. Remove cabbage. Cook garlic, ginger, and deseeded jalapeno in ghee until fragrant.
4. Return cabbage to the pot.
5. Add chicken broth and enough water to come halfway up the cabbage.
6. Braise until tender.
7. Freeze in molds.
